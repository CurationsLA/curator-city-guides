# Beverly Hills Guide

The CurationsLA Christmas and Holiday Guide to Beverly Hills

Glitz, glamour and timeless holiday magic in L.A.'s luxury enclave

Beverly Hills embodies sophisticated holiday celebrations where palm trees sparkle with designer ornaments and legendary hotels deck their lobbies with towering trees and elaborate displays. Rodeo Drive, synonymous with luxury shopping worldwide, transforms into a glittering wonderland as Nutcracker-inspired decorations adorn the palm-lined boulevard and window displays at Cartier, Gucci and Tiffany compete to dazzle passersby (rodeodrive-bh.com). The city's residential areas showcase impeccably decorated estates, while public spaces like Beverly Canon Gardens and the Lily Pond at Beverly Gardens Park host nightly light displays that draw families and visitors throughout the season (lovebeverlyhills.com). From afternoon tea at the Peninsula to fireworks over Rodeo Drive, Beverly Hills delivers holiday glamour with Southern California sunshine making outdoor celebrations accessible year-round (beverlyhills.org).

## Eat

Begin your holiday morning with breakfast at The Polo Lounge in The Beverly Hills Hotel, where Hollywood legends have gathered since 1941 for power meetings and leisurely brunches; the seasonal menu often includes festive touches and the patio dining under pink-and-green striped umbrellas feels quintessentially Beverly Hills (dorchestercollection.com). For something more casual, Nate 'n Al Delicatessen has been serving classic Jewish deli fare since 1945, with matzo ball soup, pastrami sandwiches and breakfast classics that fuel local residents and visitors alike (natenals.com).

Afternoon tea at The Peninsula Beverly Hills presents a refined holiday tradition, with finger sandwiches, scones with clotted cream and seasonal pastries served in the elegant Living Room lounge; reservations are essential during December (peninsula.com). The Belvedere at The Peninsula also offers prix-fixe holiday dinners on Christmas Eve and Day, featuring contemporary American cuisine in an intimate setting (peninsula.com).

For dinner, Spago Beverly Hills, Wolfgang Puck's flagship restaurant, serves California cuisine with Asian influences in a dining room designed by Barbara Lazaroff; holiday reservations book weeks in advance (wolfgangpuck.com). Mastro's Steakhouse provides classic American steakhouse experience with live music nightly, ideal for celebrations (mastrosrestaurants.com). Il Pastaio on Canon Drive offers Northern Italian cuisine with a charming sidewalk café atmosphere perfect for people-watching between shopping excursions (giacominodrago.com).

Don't miss The Ivy for California-Mediterranean dishes in a cottage setting adorned with white picket fences, or explore Avra Beverly Hills for Greek seafood flown in daily (theivy.com). For casual luxury, The Grill on the Alley serves American classics in a clubby atmosphere reminiscent of Manhattan steakhouses (thegrill.com). Urth Caffé on Melrose Place provides organic coffee and healthy California fare in a garden setting popular with locals (urthcaffe.com).

## Do

The Rodeo Drive Holiday Lighting Celebration typically takes place on Nov 13 2025, transforming the famous shopping street into a festive spectacle with live performances, celebrity appearances, tree lighting and fireworks drawing thousands of visitors to kick off the season (rodeodrive-bh.com). Throughout November and December, the street maintains its Nutcracker-themed decorations with twinkling lights creating magical evening shopping experiences (rodeodrive-bh.com).

Beverly Canon Gardens on Canon Drive hosts the Glowing Gardens installation from late November through early January, where hundreds of thousands of LED lights create immersive displays visitors can walk through, with synchronized music and changing color patterns (lovebeverlyhills.com). The nearby Lily Pond at Beverly Gardens Park presents Lights on the Lily Pond, another elaborate light installation that runs nightly through early January, creating a free public display perfect for family visits (beverlyhills.org).

On Dec 15 2025, the Menorah Lighting ceremony at Beverly Gardens Park celebrates Hanukkah with community gathering, music and traditional blessings, reflecting Beverly Hills' significant Jewish population (beverlyhills.org). The Virginia Robinson Gardens, a 6-acre estate garden featuring rare tropical plants, offers docent-led tours through December, showcasing California's only remaining example of the grand estates that once lined the foothills (robinsongardens.org).

The Wallis Annenberg Center for the Performing Arts presents holiday concerts, theater productions and dance performances throughout December in its state-of-the-art venues (thewallis.org). For families, the adjacent Beverly Hills Public Library often hosts seasonal storytimes and craft programs (bhpl.org).

The Paley Center for Media offers exhibitions exploring television and radio history, with special screenings of holiday classics in its intimate theater (paleycenter.org). Self-guided walking tours of the Golden Triangle shopping district reveal architectural gems and historic buildings while browsing luxury boutiques (lovebeverlyhills.com).

## Stay

The Beverly Hills Hotel, the iconic "Pink Palace" on Sunset Boulevard, has hosted Hollywood royalty since 1912, offering bungalows and rooms amid 12 acres of lush gardens, multiple pools and legendary service; holiday packages often include breakfast at The Polo Lounge and special seasonal amenities (dorchestercollection.com). The Peninsula Beverly Hills provides European elegance with impeccable service, a rooftop pool, afternoon tea service and proximity to Rodeo Drive shopping (peninsula.com).

The Beverly Wilshire, A Four Seasons Hotel occupies a prime location at the foot of Rodeo Drive, with historic architecture, luxurious rooms and the CUT steakhouse by Wolfgang Puck; film buffs recognize it as the filming location for "Pretty Woman" (fourseasons.com). Montage Beverly Hills offers contemporary luxury with a rooftop pool, full spa and residences-style accommodations in the heart of the Golden Triangle (montagehotels.com).

For boutique experiences, The Maybourne Beverly Hills (formerly The Montage) provides modern elegance with city and canyon views, while Avalon Hotel Beverly Hills presents mid-century modern design in a more intimate setting (maybournebeverlyhills.com). Mr. C Beverly Hills, from the Cipriani family, combines Italian hospitality with California style (mrchotels.com).

## Shop

Rodeo Drive anchors Beverly Hills' reputation as a luxury shopping destination, with three blocks of flagship stores from Chanel, Louis Vuitton, Hermès, Prada, Valentino and virtually every major luxury brand; holiday window displays become attractions in themselves (rodeodrive-bh.com). Two Rodeo, a European-style shopping complex featuring cobblestone streets and Baroque architecture, hosts additional boutiques including Versace and Jimmy Choo (2rodeo.com).

Beverly Drive and Canon Drive parallel to Rodeo offer more boutiques including Barney's New York, Neiman Marcus and Saks Fifth Avenue department stores providing one-stop luxury shopping (lovebeverlyhills.com). The Dec 14 2025 Holiday Craft Fair at the Beverly Hills Farmers' Market showcases local artisans offering handcrafted gifts, jewelry, art and holiday décor as an alternative to luxury retail (beverlyhills.org).

The Beverly Hills Farmers' Market operates year-round on Sundays at the Civic Center, with organic produce, flowers, prepared foods and artisan goods from certified California farmers (beverlyhills.org). For books, Taschen Store on Beverly Drive specializes in art, architecture, design and photography titles with museum-quality production (taschen.com).

The Platform in nearby Culver City (easily accessible from Beverly Hills) offers curated boutiques including Jenni Kayne and Clare V. for those seeking California-casual luxury (theplatformla.com). And while technically in West Hollywood, The Grove shopping center sits close enough for Beverly Hills visitors seeking more diverse retail options (thegrovela.com).

## Takeaway

Beverly Hills' holiday season delivers aspirational glamour grounded in genuine Southern California traditions, where you can ice skate under palm trees and sip champagne in hotel lobbies designed for old Hollywood royalty. From Rodeo Drive's designer window displays to community gatherings at Beverly Gardens Park, this legendary zip code balances exclusivity with accessibility during the most festive season. Pack elegant casual attire for upscale dining, comfortable shoes for shopping marathons and a credit card for temptations behind every gleaming storefront.
