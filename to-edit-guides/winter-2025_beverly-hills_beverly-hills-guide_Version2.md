# Beverly Hills Guide

The CurationsLA Christmas and Holiday Guide to Beverly Hills

Glitz, glamour and timeless holiday magic in L.A.’s luxury enclave

Beverly Hills softens into timeless décor and family traditions during the holidays. Palm trees on Rodeo Drive sparkle with Nutcracker-inspired ornaments and hotel lobbies glow with giant trees and festive displays.

## Eat

The Polo Lounge at The Beverly Hills Hotel and The Belvedere at The Peninsula offer prix-fixe holiday dinners and festive afternoon teas. For casual options, Royal Lobster and Pura Vida provide approachable seasonal menus.

## Do

Rodeo Drive Holiday Lighting Celebration on Nov 13 kicks off the season with performances and fireworks. Lights on the Lily Pond and Glowing Gardens at Beverly Cañon Gardens run nightly through early January.

## Stay

Historic properties like The Beverly Hills Hotel and The Peninsula offer holiday packages, festive dining and family programming.

## Shop

Rodeo Drive’s luxury boutiques and local holiday craft fairs provide upscale and artisanal gift options for shoppers.

## Takeaway

Beverly Hills turns up the holiday magic with lavish displays, elegant dining and classic events for families and visitors.
