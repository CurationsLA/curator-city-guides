# West Hollywood Guide

The CurationsLA Christmas and Holiday Guide to West Hollywood

Where glamour meets holiday sparkle in Los Angeles’ most iconic nightlife and design district

West Hollywood embraces the festive season with style. Home to the Sunset Strip, the Design District and a thriving LGBTQ+ community, the neighborhood mixes high-energy nightlife with boutique shopping and cultural events. During December the city transforms its park into an ice-skating wonderland and plays host to stylish holiday bars and philanthropic workshops.

## Eat

Celebrate Christmas Eve and Day at 1 Hotel West Hollywood. The hotel’s Yule Tide Mornings menu features gingerbread waffles paired with festive cocktails. For an indulgent dinner, Ardor at the West Hollywood Edition offers a four-course holiday feast. For casual meals, Pura Vita and Guisados provide plant-forward and Mexican comfort options.

## Do

WeHo Winter Ice Rink runs Dec 1–21 on the Great Lawn at West Hollywood Park with themed sessions and skate rentals. Winter Wonderland festival on Dec 13 is a free community celebration with crafts and snowfall. Mariah Carey’s Holiday Bar pop-up at the Mondrian Skybar opens Nov 13 for a limited seasonal run.

## Stay

Book an eco-luxe suite at 1 Hotel West Hollywood for access to festive programming. The Sunset Tower Hotel and Mondrian Los Angeles offer glamorous stays with holiday packages and rooftop events.

## Shop

The Design District hosts holiday pop-ups and workshops like Baobab’s wreath customizing. Melrose Avenue boutiques and 1 Hotel’s Goodthings Shop curate sustainable gift options.

## Takeaway

West Hollywood pairs glamour with community-focused festivities, from skating under palms to Mariah-inspired cocktails and design-led shopping.
