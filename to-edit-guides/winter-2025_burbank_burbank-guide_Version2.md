# Burbank Guide

The CurationsLA Christmas and Holiday Guide to Burbank

Studio-town cheer with family-friendly events, light displays and streaming-era nostalgia.

Burbank’s community events, studio open houses and downtown festivities make it a cheerful, convenient holiday stop for families.

## Eat

Family-friendly diners and studio cafés offer hearty holiday menus and seasonal specials near downtown Burbank.

## Do

Look for tree lightings, community parades and studio-led seasonal events; kid-focused activities are common in mid-December.

## Stay

Burbank hotels often run holiday packages aimed at visiting families and guests attending studio events.

## Shop

Local boutiques, vintage stores and shopping districts near Magnolia Park provide unique gift options.

## Takeaway

Burbank offers a cozy, family-friendly holiday experience with ties to Hollywood’s studio culture.
