# Culver City Guide

The CurationsLA Christmas and Holiday Guide to Culver City

Creative, walkable and filled with small‑business holiday cheer.

Culver City’s downtown and Culver Steps host seasonal markets, tree lightings and gallery events that celebrate neighborhood makers and creatives.

## Eat

Local favorites and farm-to-table restaurants on Main Street offer special holiday menus; bakeries and cafés serve seasonal treats for brunch.

## Do

Holiday markets at Culver City streets and neighborhood tree lightings create a festive small-town feel in the city.

## Stay

Boutique hotels near Sony Pictures and downtown make Culver City a welcoming base for holiday outings in L.A.

## Shop

Explore independent boutiques along Washington and Main for curated gifts, and check popup markets at local plazas.

## Takeaway

Culver City combines creative programming with cozy shopping and dining for a neighborly holiday experience.
