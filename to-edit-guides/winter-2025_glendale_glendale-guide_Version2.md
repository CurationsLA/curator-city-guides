# Glendale Guide

The CurationsLA Christmas and Holiday Guide to Glendale

A family-friendly holiday hub with bustling markets, festive lights and cultural traditions.

Glendale brings a neighborhood warmth to the holidays with tree lightings, shopping at Americana at Brand and community events that highlight local culture.

## Eat

Enjoy seasonal menus at The Americana’s restaurants and find comfort food favorites and bakeries around Brand Boulevard. For family brunch, local cafés on Colorado Boulevard serve festive pastries and warm beverages.

## Do

Glendale’s annual tree lighting and holiday concerts bring families together; nearby events at the Americana include seasonal installations and visits with Santa.

## Stay

Glendale hotels offer convenient access to downtown LA and family-friendly amenities; look for holiday packages and group rates during December.

## Shop

The Americana at Brand and nearby boutiques host holiday pop-ups and craft markets, perfect for local gifts and family shopping.

## Takeaway

Glendale’s holiday season emphasizes family-friendly programming, approachable dining and convenient shopping at the Americana.
