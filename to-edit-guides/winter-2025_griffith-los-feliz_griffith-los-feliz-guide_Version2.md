# Griffith Park & Los Feliz Guide

The CurationsLA Christmas and Holiday Guide to Griffith Park & Los Feliz

Green spaces, cozy neighborhood cafés and classic L.A. charm for a nature-forward holiday.

This area blends outdoor adventures in Griffith Park with indie shops and low-key holiday programming along Los Feliz Marketplace and Vermont Avenue.

## Eat

Neighborhood bakeries and cafés in Los Feliz serve holiday pastries and seasonal beverages; family restaurants offer comforting dinners.

## Do

Explore Griffith Park’s seasonal hikes, the Griffith Observatory views at sunset, and local community tree lightings and performances.

## Stay

Boutique inns and nearby hotels provide quaint, walkable stays with easy access to park trails and Los Feliz dining.

## Shop

Los Feliz boutiques offer curated gifts, vinyl shops and vintage stores—perfect for thoughtful presents.

## Takeaway

Griffith Park & Los Feliz are ideal for outdoor lovers and those seeking a quieter, local holiday pace.
