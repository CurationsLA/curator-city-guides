# Long Beach Guide

The CurationsLA Christmas and Holiday Guide to Long Beach

A waterfront holiday destination with boat parades, light displays and family-friendly attractions.

Long Beach’s waterfront neighborhoods stage boat parades and host holiday markets and performances spanning the waterfront to Retro Row.

## Eat

Seafood restaurants along the waterfront serve seasonal menus; Retro Row’s diners and cafés offer casual comfort food and holiday specials.

## Do

Don’t miss Long Beach’s holiday boat parades and Waterfront winter events, plus local theater performances and markets.

## Stay

Waterfront hotels and boutique stays offer easy access to the pier, aquarium and holiday lights across the city.

## Shop

Browse Retro Row and local night markets for vintage finds, crafts and gifts from local makers.

## Takeaway

Long Beach blends maritime traditions with neighborhood markets and family-focused holiday programming.
